My Advice to People in Corporate Tech Jobs

Article is a continuation to Quora thread on Corporate Jobs

There is some advice, tricks & etiquette on the thread mentioned above. Here is my three pints of advice.

Pint 1: Learn some software estimation in a team setting

  • It’s complex topic. It’s going to be a math with project scope and man-hours.
  • Get familiar with methodologies like Agile.

To cut the topic short:

  • Break the project functionality into tasks & sub tasks.
  • Put story points for each task.
  • Convert story points to man-hours.

That is how you arrive at a number. Add decent buffer time to the number.

Never start a software project without deadline. It will almost never get done or it is unimportant.

Pint 2: Learn to communicate (production) issues

  • Stay Honest and humble if you are the man behind the issue.
  • If cause is unknown, discuss with the lead and prioritize it.

Pint 3: Make process leaner, easier and organized

  • Automate the routine/error prone stuff.
  • Setup analytics in the business app. None of the business app should ever go on-line without analytics.
  • Organize some code or book shelf or something at work. At least maintain a clean desk.

You can break the rules but respect the law.
