Webuser magazine Jan 2018 edition - my highlights

This is a new series of blog posts where I will be sharing the top 3 things from the magazine I completed reading recently.

Webuser is all about – find more on wiki, let me keep the blog post short and sweet. I am not paid to write blog posts. Its not even a part of my KPI.

Top 1 - Tab pages

When you open a new tab in chrome, a nice fancy message or something else might greet you. Its called tab pages. It a chrome extension. Most popular of them is Momentum

The article nicely listed the most popular tab pages and I was fascinated by the idea of tab pages. Soon will roll out mine. It will be related to book of the day.

Soon expect a tab page from me, related to books.

Top 2 - Sharing prime account with family

Last year Reliance Jio created ripples in the youth community. This year its amazon prime, everyone needs it to buy goods and watch videos. Sharing prime account means sharing username and password in India. Webuser shows how a family can make use of prime by sharing it officially with family members. This is something I have not found in amazon India website.

I found this cool as it reduces hustle and adds value to the customer.

Top 3 - Ad, Computer active sneak preview

Advertising in magazines is a tricky thing, if you get it wrong. You may lose your customers. Web user has provided a nice sneak preview to their sister product Computer active magazine which is quite relevant for a geek like me.

That’s it.. Many more magazine highlights to follow.
