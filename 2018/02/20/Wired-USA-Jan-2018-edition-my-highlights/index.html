Wired USA Jan 2018 edition - my highlights

This post related to the highlights from the magazine I last completed reading.

Top 1 - Robot friendly homes

Lets acknowledge the fact that Robots will make way to our homes. We need to plan our homes accordingly. For example, a door knob shall be replaced by sensor based entry or a button. The homes will have provision to enable machines to move around easily. Its not vaastu, child safe but also robot friendly that needs to be considered.

Top 2 - Caring for old parents via App

Caring for parents who choose to live away from you is a tough job. You miss out on the monitoring and support. Some people install cameras but it needs a human to watch them to confirm that they are doing well.

An app puts a virtual pet in a smart phone or tab. It talks with them, shows them pictures and listens to them. If they don’t respond, it will notify you.

Complete article here on wired

Top 3 - Ad, Bose headphone with Google assistant.

Well presented with a nice tag line. Liked it. It goes something like this “what ever you listen, listen it better”.
