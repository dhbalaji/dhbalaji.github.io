Web designer UK Jan 2018 edition - my highlights

This post related to the highlights from the magazine I last completed reading.

Top 1 - Wonderful design inspiration from web

This magazine inspires the designers with lot of samples from live websites. It also shows real code with HTML, CSS where ever necessary. For example, chasing circles loader was demonstrated from code. Reading this magazine is like attending a 3-day workshop at your home.

Top 2 - 51North case study

Most designers would dream of starting up or being on their own. This section of the magazine is for the entrepreneurship oriented designers. 51North is a design studio who are around 9 people and been operating close to 8 years. The case study talks about their culture, project selection criteria, tools, team and tech used.

51North tries to hit the balance instead of running to hit the most numbers on website audits. They have also developed a Progressive web applications. That is their take. They are a design first companies.

Top 3 - Ad - website first, product second

If you need to put your ad on this website, I guess you need a chic website which the design community might find it useful. I liked all their ads because they are so inspiring.
