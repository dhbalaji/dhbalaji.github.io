5 Magazines and Podcasts I read/listen to

These are the 5 magazines I read. I have read more than 15 different magazines and shortlisted the below ones. I am not open to reading any other magazines as I have shortlisted these after scrutiny.

  1. The economist

    Printed in USA, it covers the overall economic state of many countries in the world including India. Short and precise read.

  2. The web user

    Printed in UK, it covers all the apps and other resources.

  3. The web designer

    Printed in UK, it covers design to delivery of web sites. It highlights very creative web projects undertaken along with the latest in web tech.

  4. The wired USA

    Printed in USA, it contains articles all about tech.

  5. The open source magazine

    Printed in India, it covers articles catering developer to CTO. Quite useful to keep up with ever changing tech.

5 podcasts I listen to on iTunes

> Podcasts are generally informative articles recorded and distributed as mp3 files over the internet

  1. Dean Bhokari meaningful show

    Covers mostly non-fiction book talks and author interviews. Sometimes author shares some lifestyle related advice too.

  2. The web platform

    Covers podcasts and guest interviews on all articles related to the web.

  3. Tools day

    Podcast on mostly frontend tech. Little bit informal in nature.

  4. Hansel minutes

    Podcast on articles related to web technologies mostly AI, ML etc.

  5. The Tai Lopez Show

    Mostly a self help and business podcast.

iTunes desktop is by far the best podcast client I know of.
