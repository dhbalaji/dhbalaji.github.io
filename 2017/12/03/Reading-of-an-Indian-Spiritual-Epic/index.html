Reading the great Indian Epic - The Mahabharatha

Hi Guys, as far as the reading goal of this year is concerned. I hit the nail on its head.

I decided to read 30 normal books related to personal development, interpersonal skills etc. The list is available here on Good reads

The aim was to finish reading the 30 books quickly and move on to a heavy read.

The goal was met in September. I had 3 months time and I had to pick a heavy read. I picked Mahabharata: Greatest epic of all time

Here is my self talk about the book.

How did I come to know about the book

Partly I must give the credit to the TV. Without TV I would not have come across such epic. I always wanted to read and find out in depth about this subject. It also talks about Lord Krishna as a central character which also motivated me.

How was the experience

  • First few chapters were quite hazy in outline. I was new to this genre of books which use the words like celestial, Higher planets, asceticism etc. It was bit hard to get started

  • After a while, I was pulled into by the characters and the plot of the story.

  • Overall it was a wow experience with some intangible learning

Was it light/heavy read

Undoubtedly epics cannot be light, this one was heavy. I had the Audio book format from audible which made things different. Probably audio book is the best for books like this one. Its nice to get moved away by story.

It took around 2 months of intermittent reading to complete this one.

How is the book organized

Broken down to 2 parts and 86 chapters in total

Interesting parts of the book

The War and the problems faced while contending with each other still playing it fairly.

Do you recommend this read

Of course, why not

About author and presentation skills

The author is Krishna Dharma. This is the first book that I had read from this author. Language is rich and picturesque.

Some topics are trimmed down to bare essentials. Bhagavad gita is a single chapter in this book. This one topic alone would fill a part of this book but the author had considered not to do so. That set the right tone for the reader.

Celestial weapons and warfare are very well presented in words.

How to get most of this read

Read it under the supervision of an spiritual expert. Ask your doubts and clarify at the earliest. Some topics are not so obvious.

Where can I get this book

Wherever books are sold. Search for the name
