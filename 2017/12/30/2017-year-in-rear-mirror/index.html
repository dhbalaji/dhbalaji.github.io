2017, year in Review

I am writing this post on December 30, 2017. It is the last weekend of this year. My colleagues are already enquiring about my new year plans.

Let me evaluate 2017 in these 3 areas.

  • Health
  • Wealth
  • Spiritual

Health

BAD

  • Workouts were less than 3 hours per month.
  • Sitting for long hours.
  • Bad posture.

Good

  • Did not binge on food.
  • Had good stamina for workout or for run.
  • Health tests shared happy news so internally I am good.

Wealth

Bad

  • Wedding & wedding loans. Did not repay loan on time due to misinformation. Never trust the intermediate guy.
  • Wealth on display attracts trespassers.

Good

  • Learnt to handle funds while taming the financial elephant.
  • Controlled shopping.
  • Embracing minimalism.
  • Learnt to sell unused goods on olx.

Career was stable and had no bad remarks. Started this blog.

Spiritual

Bad - almost none

Good

  • Controlled ego, still temper is triggered at times
  • Read 40 books which included a few spiritual books
  • Had shown more devotion and offered more prayers
  • Gave away charity generously

Verdict

Stable year. Adult life is showing its colours.

Learning to pursue Virtue, Profit and Pleasure simultaneously.

Handling sad news is part of adult life. It could be demise, insult, rejection or failure. Use your spiritual knowledge to console yourself. Its your duty to pick yourself up and keep moving through life. If you are sad, its your problem. If something is bothering you, seek counsel from wise one.

Social pressure and office politics exist on earth.

Music failed to entertain me, luckily I found Quora, podcasts etc.

Proud purchases - Car, Laptop (this is more useful than car) and lot of books.

Learn to communicate with yourself and others. Important skill for adults.
